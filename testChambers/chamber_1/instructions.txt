Basic usage.

Go to 'a', open a portal there with 'pBlue'
Go to 'b', open a second portal there with 'pOrange'
Use cd on the second portal to come back to 'a'
Use mv to push the cube through the portal to 'b'
Use 'pWhere' to see where both portals are.
Use 'pClose' to close both portals.
Use 'pWhere' again.
